Hi there! I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence School, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I am advised by Zhanyu Ma, Yunpeng Jia, Heng Guo and Boxin Shi.

I earned my B.S. degree from the School of Information and Communication Engineering, BUPT in Jun. 2022.

My research interest includes 3D reconstruction and polarized-based vision.
